FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You asked and we answered.

+ What can I upload?
PDFs, lecture slides, textbooks, articles, web links, videos, and podcasts. If you learn from it, Mindgrasp can process it.
+ What do I get back?
Clean notes and summaries, auto flashcards and quizzes, and an AI tutor that answers questions grounded only in your uploads. You also get progress signals to see what to review next.
+ How accurate is it?
Outputs are generated from your files and citations. It handles core facts well and improves with clearer source material. For high-stakes work, double-check key points like you would with any study aid.
+ Is my data private?
Your content is stored securely and used only to provide and improve the service. You stay in control of what you upload.
+ Does it integrate with my tools?
You can connect Mindgrasp with other tools to streamline your study workflow and keep everything in one place.
